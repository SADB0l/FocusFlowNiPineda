What is FocusFlow?

FocusFlow is a system software designed to enhance learning, time management, and self-study for students and professionals. It features flashcards for efficient memorization and review, allowing users to create and save their own sets. Additionally, it includes a Pomodoro Timer, which helps structure work sessions with scheduled breaks to maintain focus and prevent burnout. Users can customize both the work duration and break times to suit their preferences.
